CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

PEGGY A. SAANUO

Peggy is the chief executive officer of Vybra Consult Ltd. She specializes in International Business Development and Project Management; and has extensive experience in health, and health policy management. Since Vybra Consult’s founding, she has worked on projects around the world, with an emphasis on Africa and North America. She is based in Accra and, with her team, provides transformative growth and development to businesses and stakeholders, responding quickly, strategically and decisively to their most complex and critical challenges. In her more than 10 years of experience, Peggy has combined her skills in business turnaround, strategic consulting, and stakeholder management in the development of performance improvement and accelerated transformation projects for many large and high-profile businesses. Before joining Vybra Consult, Peggy was the Operations Manager, Business Development Manager, and Medical Representative for many multinational and prestigious local companies. She is also the TV Host for “Ask Your Pharmacist” Health show on TV3, GHONE, ETV etc…


Peggy is a graduate of Providence College (USA) and the University of Ghana Business School. Long involved in philanthropic causes, she is the founder of The Women of Wonders (WOW) Foundation, she serves in a personal capacity on the board of the Global Sustainable Aid Project (GSAP), the advisory board of Dr. Steve Mecca Foundation and as a trustee of the Women’s Trust Organization.

“For over a decade, Peggy has helped healthcare leaders bring to life performance improvement solutions and revenue cycle transformation initiatives.”
